# Ivey HBA + Engineering vs U of T Engineering Science - Career Guide 2026

## Quick Recommendation

**For most students in 2026: Choose U of T Engineering Science**

| Factor | Ivey HBA + Engineering | U of T EngSci |
|--------|------------------------|---------------|
| Time | 5 years (2 degrees) | 4 years (1 degree) |
| Cost | ~$74K CAD | ~$60-80K CAD total |
| Starting Salary | $70-85K CAD (avg) | $75-95K CAD |
| AI Resilience | 4/5 stars | 5/5 stars |

**Why U of T for AI Era:**
1. Technical depth matters more than ever (AI automating entry-level work)
2. Business skills can be added later (MBA, certificates)
3. Technical depth is harder to acquire (takes years)
4. Cost efficiency ($50K+ savings)
5. AI/ML skills command $50K+ salary premium

**Choose Ivey If:**
- Certain about consulting/finance/business career
- Value Canadian business network
- Cost not primary concern
- Want faster track to leadership

## Bottom Line

**For AI era (2026+): U of T Engineering Science is the safer, more flexible choice**

- Technical depth = foundation (harder to add later)
- Business skills = learnable (MBA, experience, certificates)
- AI premium = $50K+ for technical AI skills
- Cost savings = $50K+ to invest elsewhere

**Ivey makes sense only if:**
- You're certain about business career
- Network value outweighs cost
- You learn better through case studies

Both paths can lead to success. What matters more:
- Your drive and work ethic
- Continuous learning (especially AI)
- Network-building skills
- Judgment and decision-making
- Willingness to adapt
