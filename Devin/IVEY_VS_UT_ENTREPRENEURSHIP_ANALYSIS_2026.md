## 📊 Part 8: Risk-Adjusted Return Analysis

### Expected Value Calculation

**Ivey + HBA Entrepreneurship Path:**
```
Probability of Success (moderate exit, $20M): 10%
Probability of Failure: 75%
Probability of Lifestyle Business: 15%

Expected Value:
= (10% × $20M) + (75% × $0) + (15% × $2M)
= $2M + $0 + $0.3M
= $2.3M expected value

Time Investment: 5 years school + 5-7 years startup
Opportunity Cost: ~$500K (lost salary)
Net Expected Value: ~$1.8M
```

**U of T EngSci Entrepreneurship Path:**
```
Probability of Success (deep tech exit, $100M): 12%
Probability of Failure: 70%
Probability of Lifestyle Business: 18%

Expected Value:
= (12% × $100M) + (70% × $0) + (18% × $3M)
= $12M + $0 + $0.54M
= $12.54M expected value

Time Investment: 4 years school + 6-9 years startup
Opportunity Cost: ~$400K (lost salary)
Net Expected Value: ~$12.1M
```

**Key Insight:** U of T EngSci has ~6.7x higher expected value for tech entrepreneurship, but with longer time horizon and higher variance.
